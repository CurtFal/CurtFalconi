The first entry!

This is my first time doing this kind of thing and holy SHIT is there a lot to write about just for these past 2 days.
Let's talk about the intersting things though, why am I doing this? Aren't blogs overdone? Isn't all this just meaningless garbage that could probably be posted on twitter?

Yea, probably. But posting things on twitter at 12 am on a tuesday isn't quite as much fun.

I was pretty told I have no hours in the future at my job which is well, uhhh, concerning. I really relied on that job to get me cash so now I have no idea what I'm going to do. This fucking Caronavirus shit has made my life harder in so many goddamn ways. I have about 50 hours of vaction time which is approx. 2 weeks of pay, so I should be good for the next ~4 weeks due to our staggered paychecks, but I'm FUCKED after that. Maybe this really will turn into the apocalypse and money won't be a concern in 2-4 months, but I wouldn't hold out hope. If you are reading this from the future like some goddamn video game found note, then congrats, I'm doing that annoying fucking trope rn.

ANYWAYS, I'm doing this because I found out like 12 hours ago that I don't have a steady job for the time being, it's supossed to be a work night but I don't have shifts anymore, so I'm writing websites at 12am and I'm like 2 beers in. Is this a good idea? no. absolutely not. I cannot hold my alcohol in any capacity. but in the absence of any other good ideas I'll hold this path. It's more productive than just playing videos games.... probably.
I could probably be applying for unemployment right around now but that's a SHIT ton of paperwork and I am not in the mental state to handle that in this current moment. I don't want to talk about work too much on here because
. a. it's boring
. b. I don't want this coming back to bite me in the ass
but, these are difficult times so what better time to start a blog!!!????
The caronavirus has gotten to the point where we have a curfew in place between 8pm and 5am in NJ so that's nice, but there's the threat of liquor stores closing and my inner alcoholic just can't accept that. The GOOD news is that the new Animal Crossing comes out on Friday and we get Half Life: Alyx on Monday. To be honest this is kind of the best time for a quarantine to happen if such a thing exists. The only annoying thing is CyberPunk 2077 getting delayed because I was really looking forward to playing that all summer, but those plans are shot now.

To be completely honest, I have no idea what is going to happen. I am on spring break but my professor for comp lab tech and web dev has told us ZERO plans for online courses. That is concerning to say the least. Hell I don't even give a shit about my programming workshop or CyberSecurity, those classes were a chore. But I do care about finishing school. I want to be done so badly and have a full time job and yet here we are worrying about a fucking super flu. I know the website LostZombies.com got abandoned, but their timeline is matching up pretty much EXACTLY with our current COVID-19 crisis. Life imitates art style. If people start eating eachother in September then it's proven that those guys were just illuminatti the entire time.

I have no idea what I'm going to do with this blog nor do I expect anyone to read it. I think this is just my way of venting fustration and the possibility of someone reading this garbage is perhaps better for us all.
The world is very fucking scary right now and I don't know what the next 1,2,3,6, or 12 months look like, so I'm just going to keep writing websites and playing video games to keep sane. I imagine this site will change pretty drastically over the coming weeks in terms of layout, but we shall see.

Until then, seeya
-Curt
